    • Translation Feature

    The interactive Peace Card includes distinctive symbols, along with a unique QR code that allows visitors to instantly access the voice translation service. This service enables visitors to communicate easily and effectively with others regardless of language barriers, as it converts texts or symbols into instant voice translation in the visitor's language. This smart solution helps facilitate requesting assistance and provides a safer and more comfortable experience within the Grand Mosque.

    Smart Prayer Rug

    The Smart Prayer Rug of Mecca is an innovative part of the project, featuring built-in smart QR codes that allow visitors to locate the nearest available rug within the Grand Mosque. Visitors can scan the QR code using a dedicated app or rely on smart color signals to guide them to available prayer rug areas with ease. The smart rug aims to enhance the prayer and movement experience inside the mosque, promoting comfort and organization in the holy places.
